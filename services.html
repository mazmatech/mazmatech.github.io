Solution Station-service.

This solution allows oil companies to issue and manage various services to their customers, including mobile refills. In addition, the "My Station" card, which is easy to use, offers an efficient and practical management system in terms of deployment, which can easily evolve by integrating, for example, the sale of automotive products (gasoline, oil change, lubricants, etc.).

Solution kiosque

This solution consists in acquiring refills via an interactive terminal installed in a high-traffic area, the interactive terminal provides access to one or more services while reinforcing communication with the customer thanks to its 24-hour availability in public places.

Mazma allows its customers to recharge their mobiles via ATMs, but also to consult their banking data and carry out transactions through their smart-phone.
